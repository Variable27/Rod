NISU

Northern Iloilo State University

NISU is committed to academic excellence, community service, and the advancement of technology and innovation. As a leading institution in the region, NISU empowers students with knowledge and skills to address the challenges of the modern world.

The College of Information and Computing Studies (CICS) endeavors to:

  • Train students to become computer professionals knowledgeable and competent in meeting the growing demand for IT professionals in government, business, industry, and education.
  • Undertake relevant ICT research to provide valuable services and share expertise with stakeholders for the benefit of the local, regional, and national community.
  • Inculcate positive Filipino values, attitudes, and lifelong learning practices in students.

Program Objectives for Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

  • Acquire computing concepts and theories, algorithmic foundations, and new developments in computing to design and develop software solutions.
  • Prepare students with practicum programs, field exposures, and learning experiences for future computing needs.
  • Engage in research and collaborative work with the industry for practical applications of computing technologies.
  • Transfer technology through extension activities to improve community lives.
  • Develop teamwork, interpersonal skills, and social responsibilities for local, regional, and national development.
